Max Temperature

Through the analysis, we see the relationship that the degrees becomes higher as we get closer to the equator. The equator is represented by 0 degree latitude on the x axis. The southern hemisphere is represented by the negative degree latitudes and the northern hemisphere is represented by the positive degree latitudes. In this case, the cities in the southern hemisphere are warmer than the northern hemisphere. It may have been winter in the northern hemisphere and summer in the southern hemisphere at the time the data was pulled.
