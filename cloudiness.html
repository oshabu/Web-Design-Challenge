Cloudiness

In this section, we see the relationship that it is generally less cloudy as we get closer to the equator. The equator is represented by 0 degree latitude on the x axis. The southern hemisphere is represented by the negative degree latitudes and the northern hemisphere is represented by the positive degree latitudes. It is interesting to note that more cities in the northern hemisphere are less cloudy further from the equator than cities in the southern hemisphere. However, there are also significantly more cloudy cities in the northern hemisphere than the southern hemisphere. This may be due to the time of year the data was pulled and the jet streams pushing more moisture and clouds in northern hemisphere cities further from the equator
