Wind Speed

In this part, we see the relationship that it is less windy as we get closer to the equator. The equator is represented by 0 degree latitude on the x axis. The southern hemisphere is represented by the negative degree latitudes and the northern hemisphere is represented by the positive degree latitudes. On average, a majority of the cities in the analysis have a wind speed of 10mph or less. More cities in the northern hemisphere have less wind further from the equator than the southern hemisphere. Cities with the highest wind are in the northern hemisphere and far from the equator.
