Humidity

For this part, we see the relationship that it is generally more humid as we get closer to the equator. The equator is represented by 0 degree latitude on the x axis. The southern hemisphere is represented by the negative degree latitudes and the northern hemisphere is represented by the positive degree latitudes. The wind patterns and jet streams at the time of year the data was pulled could be blowing in a way that more moisture was being sent further from the equator in the northen hemisphere leading us to see more humidity further from the equator in the northern hemisphere.
